Feed Me Properly

A rules-based nutrition guide for real life, imperfect memory, and meals that arrive as a blur of coffee, yoghurt, wraps, and whatever the day coughed up.

This version goes further than generic patter. It scores likely meal patterns, explains why they matter, and pairs the results with practical guidance inspired by trusted Australian nutrition resources.

            How this version works

            • It matches your text against a food library with rough nutrient estimates for common foods.
            • It scores likely protein, fibre, iron, calcium, fruit and vegetable coverage, and steadiness of energy support.
            • It injects short guidance summaries tied to Australian nutrition resources instead of just dumping a link and fleeing the scene.
            • It stays transparent about uncertainty rather than pretending your vague pizza memory came with a laboratory report.

            This tool is still heuristic, not clinical. Its job is to be useful, not theatrical.

            Feed Me Properly is a work in progress.

            It is for general nutrition guidance and practical next-step thinking, not diagnosis or personalised medical care.
