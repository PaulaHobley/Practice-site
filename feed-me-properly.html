Feed Me Properly

A rules-based nutrition guide for real life, vague meal descriptions, and days where the food situation is being held together with yoghurt, caffeine, and optimism.

Paste what you ate, choose your main goal, add any relevant filters, and the tool will give you a practical read on likely strengths, likely gaps, useful next steps, and trusted Australian resources.

            How it works

            • It scans your text for known foods and drinks.
            • It estimates nutritional patterns rather than pretending it knows exact quantities.
            • It changes its suggestions based on your selected goal and context filters.
            • It points you to trusted Australian resources when those are more useful than a guess wrapped in confidence.

            This tool is intentionally rules-based. No black-box nutrition oracle. Just transparent logic with a decent bedside manner.

            Feed Me Properly is a work in progress.

            It is for general nutrition guidance and practical next-step thinking, not diagnosis or personalised medical care.
